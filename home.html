Human Vibrations


Human vibration refers to the mechanical oscillations or movements that the human body is exposed to, often in an occupational or environmental context. These vibrations can be transmitted to the human body through various sources, and they can have both beneficial and harmful effects. Human vibration is an interdisciplinary field that involves biomechanics, occupational health, and engineering. Human vibration, which encompasses both whole-body vibration (WBV) and hand-arm vibration (HAV), is a critical consideration in various industries and working environments.
